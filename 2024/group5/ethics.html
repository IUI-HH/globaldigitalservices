Data Privacy and Transparency

It is critical to maintain transparency about how learner data is used and to protect the privacy of users to prevent misuse of information.

Fairness and Bias

AI systems must be designed to avoid biases that may affect the learning material, ensuring fairness regardless of language, culture, or demographic.

Accessibility and Digital Divide

While AI can enhance accessibility, it also has the potential to widen the digital divide if not made equally available to all learners.

Impact on Employment

The integration of AI in language learning must consider its impact on educators and the potential displacement of jobs.
