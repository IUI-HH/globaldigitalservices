Concerns of AI in the Classroom

Cheating

Effects of AI on Cheating

According to Pewresearch, 1 in 5 students have used ChatGPT for their schoolwork. Many educators view the use of AI written content as plagiarized. According to Business Insider, this has led to an increase of documented cheating and an increase of chastised students.

Biases in AI

Explaining inherent biases in AI

Although AI tends to be depicted in media as thinking for itself, the program itself is only as smart as the one who created it. Waldon University explains that AI is not something to be feared, but rather something to be wary about due to the biases that a program may have. These biases will be based on what information the AI has been given.

Inaccurate Information

How AI Misguides Users

AI may source information from unreliable online content, integrating inaccuracies into educational resources. Tech for America explains how most AI systems go to large search engines such as Google for information to write about.

Isolation

AI's Impacts on Social Skills

Increased reliance on AI for learning tasks can lead to reduced human interaction and collaboration among students. As students begin to interact with AI for projects and essays, there becomes less of a need to go to outside sources or partners for any form of collaboration, as explained by Waldon University.
